# Footer
The footer provides copyright, licensing and other information such as social media links.

In most cases, the footer is made up of two components.
1. [The actual footer](./basic-footer) (referred to as `Basic Footer` or `Regular Footer`)
2. [The our sites section](./our-sites) that includes links to other Government websites

To learn more about the individual components, click the links above. Below is some examples of the complete footer.

### Regular footer
This is used throughout gov.je, blog.gov.je, one.gov.je, etc. It is not used on the gov.je home page.
